Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. General Policy

Due to the nature of digital services, payments made for completed or partially completed services are non-refundable unless otherwise agreed in writing.

2. Project-Based Services

Refund requests must be made before project initiation. Once work has commenced, refunds may not be applicable. Milestone-based payments are non-refundable once the milestone is delivered.

3. Marketing & Advertising Services

Performance marketing and advertising services involve external platform variables. Service fees are non-refundable once campaigns are launched or strategies are implemented.

4. Cancellation

Clients may cancel ongoing services with written notice. Any outstanding dues must be settled prior to termination.

5. Exceptional Circumstances

In rare and justified cases, GoSparkMedia may evaluate partial refunds at its sole discretion.
